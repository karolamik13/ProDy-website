Water Bridge Finder

WatFinder detects protein-water interactions (water bridges or water-mediated associations) relevant to protein stability and dynamics.

WatFinder can analyze single protein structures (PDB files), collections of structures (PDB ensembles), and molecular dynamics trajectories to detect water molecules that form water bridges with protein structures. It uses multiple different criteria, including distances and angles. The code is flexible and allows the user to tune the data analysis appropriately to the analyzed system.
WatFinder provides a suite of functions for generating raw data and outputs on statistical analyses and visualization tools.

Features

You can use WatFinder for

  • Identifying water molecules in contact with the protein structure
  • Detecting pairs of residues linked by water bridges
  • Determining prevalent water-attracted regions within the protein structure, i.e., water clusters (based on PDB Ensemble or trajectory)
  • Generating histograms displaying the frequency of water molecules involved in specific water bridges
  • Analyzing the frequency or probability of water bridges occurring between residue pairs
